  2. Microsoft® Windows 10 (64-bit)
  3. 8 GB of RAM (at least 16 GB recommended)
  4. 5 GB of available hard disk space for installation; additional free space required during installation (cannot install on flash drives)
  5. Screen resolution of 1280×1080 (1920×1080 or higher recommended)
